# Aura CRM - Demo Video Transcript (Male Voice)
**Duration:** 5 minutes 30 seconds

---

### Scene 1: Landing
Welcome to Aura, the AI-native CRM built from the ground up for direct-to-consumer brands. In today's market, customer data is your single most valuable asset, but it's often trapped in disconnected tools. Aura unifies everything, predicts customer behavior, and automates multi-channel marketing. Let's step inside and take a comprehensive tour.

### Scene 2: Dashboard
This is your command center. The KPI cards at the top give you an instant pulse: Total Revenue, Active Customers, and Average Order Value. Below, interactive charts track revenue trends and customer growth over time. Hover over any data point for granular, day-by-day insights. This is real-time, dynamic reporting that helps you spot trends and anomalies the moment they happen.

### Scene 3: Customers
The Customers directory gives you a unified view of every shopper. You can search and filter by city, spend, or tags. Clicking a customer reveals their full profile: lifetime value, order history, last purchase date, and automatically applied behavioral tags like VIP or At-Risk. This gives your team complete context before every interaction.

### Scene 4: Segments Creation
Now let's create a customer segment. We click New Segment, and a creation modal opens. First, we switch to AI-Assisted mode. Instead of manually configuring filters, we simply describe what we want in plain English. Let's type: Find customers who haven't purchased in 60 days but have spent over 5000 rupees. We click Generate Rules with AI, and Aura instantly interprets our intent. Notice how it automatically populated the filter rules — minimum spend of 5000 and idle time of 60 days — and shows us that 245 customers match this criteria. It also auto-filled the segment name and description. Now we click Create Segment to save it.

### Scene 5: Segments View
And there it is. Our new segment, Lapsed High Spenders, now appears as a card on the Segments page. You can see the filter tags — minimum 5000 rupees, idle 60 plus days — along with the customer count. Each segment card is interactive and beautifully designed, showing whether it was created manually or with AI assistance.

### Scene 6: Campaign Create (Step 1)
Now let's build a campaign to re-engage our lapsed segment. In the Campaigns hub, we click New Campaign. Step 1: we name it Summer Re-engagement 2026, select our Lapsed High Spenders segment from the dropdown, and choose WhatsApp as our channel for maximum open rates. We click Continue to proceed to Step 2.

### Scene 7: Campaign Message (Step 2)
Step 2 is the message composer. Instead of writing copy from scratch, we leverage Aura's AI. We describe our campaign goal: Win back lapsed customers with an exclusive 30 percent discount. We click Generate with AI, and within seconds, Aura drafts a perfectly crafted, personalized WhatsApp message. Notice the preview below — it shows exactly what the customer will receive, with their name dynamically inserted. The copy is engaging, has a clear call to action, and includes the discount code. Now we click Review to finalize.

### Scene 8: Campaign Review & Launch (Step 3)
Step 3 is the review screen. Here we can verify every detail before going live: campaign name, target segment, channel, and the number of customers who will receive our message. The full message template is displayed below. Everything looks perfect. We click Launch Campaign, and Aura immediately begins delivering personalized WhatsApp messages to all customers in our segment.

### Scene 9: Campaign Detail
After launching, we are taken to the campaign list. Clicking on our campaign opens the detailed analytics view. Here you see six KPI cards: Total Sent, Delivered, Failed, Opened, Read, and Clicked, each with conversion percentages. Below that is the Engagement Funnel chart, visualizing the drop-off at each stage. Further down, the Message Template is displayed, followed by the full Communications Log showing every individual message, its status, and delivery timeline. This is real-time campaign intelligence at your fingertips.

### Scene 10: AI Assistant
Finally, let's meet Aura's personal AI data analyst, accessible from anywhere in the platform. We open the AI Assistant and ask: Who should I target for a re-engagement campaign? The AI instantly analyzes our store data and recommends targeting High Value Lapsed Customers, even suggesting a segment with specific filter rules. It also offers an action button to create that segment directly. Let's ask another question. We type: Draft a WhatsApp message for high-value customers. The AI generates personalized marketing copy ready to use. This is Aura CRM — beautifully designed, blazingly fast, and deeply powered by AI. Thank you for watching.
